Feed.

NUTRITION TAILORED TO YOUR PET'S NEEDS

Perfect Fit™ is expert nutrition designed to feed your pet's spirit of youth throughout their life. Developed with our vets and pet nutritionists from WALTHAM® Centre for Pet Nutrition, Perfect Fit™ combines 5 benefits addressing 5 of the most common health needs of all cats and dogs, regardless of age or lifestyle, in its unique Perfect Fit™ Total 5 formula.

Move.

ACTIVITY TO KEEP YOUR PET'S BODY HEALTHY AND FULL OF LIFE

Staying in shape requires an active lifestyle. It’s not only the key to helping your pet maintain a healthy weight and heart, but it also benefits their muscle and joint health so they can continue to be spritely and agile, even as they grow older. With our pet behaviourists, we offer advice and tools to help you ensure your pet gets the physical activity they need.

Play.

GAMES TO HELP KEEP YOUR PET HAPPY AND ENGAGED

Playtime helps build that special loving bond between you and your pet, but it also essential for your pet’s mental development. Stimulating their mind through play, whether on their own or with you, will help to fuel their youthful spirit, keeping them happy every day of their life. That’s why we aim to provide intuitive tools and innovative ways to help you keep their mind active!
