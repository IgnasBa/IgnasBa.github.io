ABOUT

COMPANY

ScandiMate is a company providing work and travel services for skilled Danish craftsman who would like to try their hand in Australia and New Zealand. The company offers help for these future employees in every aspect, such as housing, visa or transfer from the airport and most importantly the work opportunity itself. Because of these facts, the collaboration with construction companies is perfect for both sides. We’ll find a job for our customers and your company will get ScandiMate’s mediator service for free in exchange. The only paper our customers need for their knowledge is a one-month contract.

OWNER

My name is Alexander and my country of origin is Denmark, but I have lived in Scotland and Australia as well. In these countries, I learnt a lot and also gained so much experience from studying abroad. In Australia, I had a chance to build my Australian network and acquired knowledge of the construction industry in this country. PROCESS

1

Customer approaches ScandiMate.
Customer contacts ScandiMate and ScandiMate performs a background check (checks the education documents, contacts former employees and asks about reliability of the worker, attendance, etc.)

2

ScandiMate looks for job opportunities.
ScandiMate contacts construction companies and looks for the job opportunities

3

Interview with the company.
ScandiMate then books a meeting in between construction company and the customer to get to know each other and discuss important details about the job

4

ScandiMate takes care of administration tasks.
ScandiMate provides the client with housing, transportation and takes care of the necessary bureaucracy (travel insurance, bank account, etc.)

5

Customer starts to work in your company.
The client sets off to the destination and starts to work with one month trial period
