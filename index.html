Justo Gambula is a 22 year old musician.

He plays the bass as a sideman.

His music is influenced by his Tanzanian roots

Biography

Justo is originally from Tanzania and he came to Denmark with his mother. She came to Denmark because of music and her dream of opening an african camp.

When Justo was around 5 years old he had his 1st encounter with music in his father’s garage. There he found his guitar and like every curious child, he tried to see how it works.

Music was always in his family, mother singing, father playing different instruments, Justo’s older siblings playing instruments and singing as well. It was pretty natural to pick up something in this field, especially since he was 6 yo he started playing together with his family members as a way of entertainment and having a good time.” I don’t remember not playing music” - Justo Gambula.
